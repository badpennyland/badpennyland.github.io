PRICING - Custom Bleach Shirts

For a sense of pricing for your custom, hand-painted bleach shirt, add totals from steps 1, 2 and 3 below. I will always confirm that the design and pricing is exactly what you are comfortable with before starting work on the shirt. Feel free to reach out via e-mail or instagram DM for any inquiries / quote.

STEP ONE: Design
$35 - $45 (sliding)
Price depends on simplicity or complexity of design. Factors that impact pricing include (but not limited to) how simple or intricate the design is in terms of composition, placement, size and linework detail. For example, a bold minimal design will be more on the $35 end, whereas a more detailed, intricate design that will take more time for me to hand draw will be on the $45 end. However, a bold design that is scaled to fit edge to edge will be priced higher than a smaller design centered on the chest.

Exact pricing will vary - contact for quote.

STEP TWO: Shirt/Material Type
$5 - Unisex or femme fit shirt on heavy cotton
$8 - Crop top (femme xs/s or m/l sizes only)
$10 - Crewneck sweatshirt

STEP THREE: Add-Ons
$0 - No add-ons
$10 - Bleach Ringer Style (collar and sleeves are painted with bleach)
$10-20 - Design on back of shirt. Price depends on complexity, see factors detailed in step one.
