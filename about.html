About

Hey! I'm Marcea. I'm a graphic designer, screenprinter, and musician. I often screenprint as Bad Penny (Land) with myself and my partner George. I figured I would start this blog to help out other home screenprinters that want to explore it as an artistic medium. I started screenprinting about one year ago and have been through my fair share of mess ups, learning curves, and gratifying successes (both on accident and on purpose). I hope that in documenting my process, hiccups, troubleshooting, etc. I can help others who want to screenprint cheaply at home! I make under the ethics of DIY & fully support anyone who wants to learn how to do this themselves.

Feel free to leave comments on my posts, e-mail me, or follow me on Instagram. Screenprinting is fun and I hope others feel inspired to do it, too!
